# skills/shop-amazon.md — Skill: Tim kiem san pham hoc tap

> Day la vi du ve mot "skill" co the tai su dung — nhiem vu cu the Claude co the thuc hien theo yeu cau.
> Skill nay giup tim kiem sach va tai lieu hoc tieng Anh tren Amazon hoac cac nen tang thuong mai.

---

## Mo ta Skill

**Ten:** Tim tai lieu hoc tieng Anh
**Trigger:** Khi nguoi dung hoi "tim sach tieng Anh cho..." hoac "recommend tai lieu de..."
**Output:** Danh sach 3-5 tai lieu phu hop voi mo ta ngan

---

## Prompt Template

```
Tim [SO LUONG] tai lieu/sach/khoa hoc de hoc [CHU DE TIENG ANH]
phu hop voi trinh do [TRINH DO: A1/A2/B1/B2/C1/C2].

Yeu cau:
- Phu hop voi nguoi Viet Nam di lam
- Co the tim tren Amazon hoac Tiki
- Uu tien tai lieu thuc hanh (khong chi ly thuyet)
- Gia trong khoang hop ly

Tra ve:
1. Ten tai lieu + tac gia
2. Ly do phu hop
3. Link tim kiem (Amazon/Tiki)
4. Danh gia ngan (1-2 cau)
```

---

## Vi du su dung

**Input:**
> "Tim sach giao tiep van phong bang tieng Anh cho To Trinh"

**Output mau:**
1. **"Business English for Success"** — Scott McLean
   - Phu hop: Tap trung vao writing, presentations, meetings
   - Tim tren: Amazon → search "Business English Success McLean"
   - Danh gia: Sach thuc hanh, co exercises thiet thuc

2. **"Longman Business English Dictionary"**
   - Phu hop: Tu dien chuyen nganh, dung duoc hang ngay
   - Tim tren: Tiki → "Longman Business English"
   - Danh gia: Nen co trong tay, tra cuu nhanh

---
