## Ngon ngu

- **Chinh:** Tieng Viet — target users la nguoi Viet Nam
- **Phu:** Tieng Anh — chi dung cho thuat ngu chuyen nganh hoac ten khoa hoc
- **Tieu de khoa hoc:** Tieng Anh (vi do la ten chinh thuc cua khoa hoc)

---

## Giong Dieu (Tone of Voice)

**La gi:**
- Chuyen nghiep nhung than thien — nhu mot nguoi anh/chi co kinh nghiem chi bao
- Khich le, tich cuc — nhan manh co the lam duoc
- Ro rang, cu the — tranh noi chung chung
- Huong toi hanh dong — moi doan van nen dan den mot buoc tiep theo

**Khong phai:**
- Khong qua hoc thuat, cung nhan
- Khong qua thoai mai, lang nhang
- Khong negative (tranh: "kho khan", "that bai", "mat thoi gian")
- Khong oversell — loi hua phai thuc te

---

## Nguyen tac viet Headings

```
Cong thuc tot: [Hanh dong / Ket qua cu the]

Vi du tot:
"Giao tiep tu tin trong cuoc hop quoc te"
"Mo khoa co hoi nghe nghiep voi tieng Anh"
"Tu B1 len B2 trong 3 thang"

Vi du khong tot:
"Hoc tieng Anh"
"Cai thien ky nang"
"Khoa hoc tot nhat"
```

## Viet ve Benefits — Khong Features

```
Features (tranh):
"Khoa hoc co 50 bai hoc"
"Co video HD"
"Kem quiz sau moi bai"

Benefits (dung):
"Sau 50 bai hoc, ban noi duoc tieng Anh trong cuoc hop"
"Xem lai o moi luc, moi noi — tren dien thoai hoac may tinh"
"Biet chinh xac minh dang o dau — khong mat phuong huong"
```

## Khong Dung

- Jargon ky thuat ma nguoi binh thuong khong hieu
- Tieng long, viet tat khong pho bien
- So sanh tieu cuc voi doi thu
- Loi hua khong thuc te ("Dam bao thanh cong 100%")
- Dau cham than qua nhieu (!)
